Career Prediction System Using Emotional Intelligence

Students are the assets of our country. As students are going through their academics, it is very important for them to know their emotions, capabilities and identify their interests. So that they will get to know in which career area their interests and capabilities are going to put them in. In the existing system, students get career guidance and consulting from mentors or organizations, who are experts to discover which field he/she will enjoy and excel in. And most of them consider and provide guidance only based on their skillset, aptitude, social and financial status. They didn’t consider student personality or emotions.

Emotional intelligence (EI) refers to the ability to perceive, control, and evaluate emotions. Components of EI includes Empathy, Social Skill, Motivation, Self-regulation, Self-awareness. In proposed system, Emotional intelligence along with other traditional factors are considered. The website is capable of calculating EI score using questionaire to which answers can be given in the form of text and speech. Machine learning techniques are used for calculation of EI score and combine it with other traditional factors . The system help the students deciding good career for them without wasting their time and resources.

What is Emotional Inteligence ?

1996 - Salovey and Mayer’s

Emotional intelligence is the ability to process information about your own emotions and other people’s. It’s also the ability to use this information to guide your thoughts and behavior.
